Eating Commandments

  1. I. Eat between 12pm - 3pm
  2. II. Have one meal a day
  3. III. Eat only food you buy yourself
  4. IV. Your meal must consist of green vegetables and a choice of one type of fruit
  5. V. Use your hand to judge how much fruit will be in your meal by grabbing as much as you can with one hand.
  6. VI. Gather the meal and accept it before begining to eat.
  7. VII. Acknowledge what the food is.
  8. VIII. Prepare the meal ahead of time.
  9. IX. Chew the food to a pulp.
  10. X. Swallow the food as slow as possible.
These commandments, when followed will grant ultimate strength. © P.R.E.P.
